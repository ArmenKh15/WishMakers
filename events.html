Fundraising & Service Events

Wishmakers at VT hosts creative fundraising events throughout the year to support Make-A-Wish Greater Virginia and help grant local wishes.

Students work together to plan campus-based activities that build community while raising awareness of the Make-A-Wish mission.

Sample Events

  • On-campus fundraisers such as bake sales, raffles, and restaurant percentage nights that benefit Make-A-Wish Greater Virginia.
  • Mr. Wish, a signature event that combines entertainment and philanthropy to raise funds for wishes.
  • Craft days where members create cards and small gifts for children at Carilion Children’s Hospital.

How to Get Involved

  • Attend general body meetings to help brainstorm ideas and sign up for upcoming events listed through campus involvement platforms.
  • Volunteer for outreach trips and service projects that directly support wish families and local hospitals.

About Wishmakers at VT

Wishmakers at VT is a service-oriented student organization at Virginia Tech that supports the Greater Virginia chapter of the Make-A-Wish Foundation.

The club is open to students of all ages and majors and focuses on philanthropy, fundraising, and outreach that directly benefits local wish kids and families.

Why Wishes Matter

Wishes can serve as a turning point in a child’s treatment journey, boosting emotional well-being and giving families positive memories during difficult times.

Make-A-Wish and its chapters grant thousands of wishes each year, with college-based Wishmakers on Campus groups playing an important fundraising role.

Community Outreach

Members support Carilion Children’s Hospital in Roanoke, Virginia, through hands-on activities that brighten the days of young patients and their families.

Projects can include seasonal crafts and cards as well as helping with events that create a fun and encouraging environment for children receiving care.

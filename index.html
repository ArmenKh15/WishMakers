Welcome to Wishmakers at VT

Wishmakers at VT connects students of all majors and years to support the Make-A-Wish Foundation and local children with critical illnesses.

Learn More

Our Mission

The club raises funds and awareness to help grant life-changing wishes for children diagnosed with critical illnesses through Make-A-Wish Greater Virginia.

Make-A-Wish and its chapters grant wishes for eligible children, providing hope, strength, and joy during treatment and recovery.
